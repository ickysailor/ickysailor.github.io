icky sailor

I'm Nikki, and I'm a 27 year old artist from Maryland. As a kid, I would spend countless hours watching animation, anything from a whimsical cartoon to a dramatic anime. My summer nights would consist of me filling up a sketchbook or drawing on my PC until 6am. Not only did I enjoy drawing, but I found a love for designing websites and coding profiles. I have created several website, blog, and profile templates.

I have a passion for design, and I have the skill to create what you need. This includes (but is not limited to) newsletters, logos, icons, infographics, website designs, posters, and user-friendly interface. I will maintain consistent use of your brand identity.

How about an illustration that pops? Vector or raster, I can create art with permanent use that stands out. Package design, book illustration, or character portrait—I am capable of delivering art that suits your needs. My dream is to spend the rest of my life doing what I love: being creative.
